Class: BookingRequest

BookingRequest(clientUserID, dateEnd, dateStart, hostUserID, listingID, needMover, requestState, clientAddress, clientAddress, inventoryPhotoURL, inventoryList)

Holds information regarding a booking request made by a client for a particular listing held by a host.

Constructor
